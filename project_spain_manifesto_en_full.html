PROJECT SPAIN

Manifesto of the Common Spaniard

1. Defense and Borders

Spain must regain control. Not only of its borders, but of its dignity. Strengthening the southern frontier with resolve is essential.

2. Hispanic Alliance

Latin America is not foreign to us. It is our history and our future. We promote a real union between Hispanic American states.

3. Liberty, Not Subsidies

Full respect for identity, gender, and religion. But the State does not subsidize beliefs or personal decisions. Being of any chosen gender carries no public economic compensation.

4. End to Political Corruption

Lifetime pensions for politicians are abolished. Being a public representative is an honor, not a privilege. The size of the political apparatus is reduced.

5. Symbolic Monarchy

We recognize the diplomatic role of the Royal House. It is maintained as a military honorary symbol and High National Diplomatic Representation.

6. Simplification of the State

Complete audit of public spending. Useless expenditures are eliminated. Goodbye to advisors and positions nobody understands.

7. Useful Education

Only degrees with real market demand will be subsidized. Public education must cease to be ideological and become practical again.

8. Rational Subsidies

Only two: basic income and unemployment benefits. The minimum income is conditional on training and active job search.

9. Real Justice

Harsher sentences, removal of prison privileges. New prisons built under minimum legal conditions but strict regimes.

10. End of Squatting

A residence is legal only if you are the owner or have a rental contract. Social cases are relocated. Criminals go to jail. Illegal immigrants are deported.

11. Catalonia and the Basque Country

They are part of Spain, with their culture and language. But privileges and political blackmail are over. A national referendum will be proposed if necessary. Those who want to leave will be told the truth. Those who stay are my brothers and sisters.

12. National Energy

Nuclear plants remain active. New ones will be considered. Spain will no longer depend on foreign energy. Total energy autonomy.

13. Real Defense

Increased defense budget, reinforced southern border, modernized army. Spain demands respect.

14. Responsible Civil Service

Real performance reviews. Those who serve stay. Those who don't, are dismissed.

15. Healthcare with Purpose

More direct funding for doctors, nurses, and real medical staff. Wasteful spending is over.

16. Sovereign Agriculture

Fair prices, national logistics, and equal conditions for national producers versus imports.

17. EU with Respect

Spain is unquestionably part of the EU. We demand support to control the southern border alongside Portugal and Italy. Gibraltar is Spanish—and thus European.

18. NGOs and Subsidies

All aid is audited. Only causes that benefit the collective interest are funded. First, we help our homeless and hungry.

19. Technology as an Ally

Spain embraces AI, automation, and strong tech alliances. Big Tech friendly—but under national digital sovereignty. Technology must serve the people.

There is still much to define and develop, but these are basic ideas that most Spaniards should agree upon.

You may agree with some points and disagree with others—fair enough. But think about what you've lived through:

A state that only works when nothing is happening. What happened during the floods in Valencia? During COVID? During blackouts? They’re useless in crisis. The system DOES NOT WORK.

Who was responsible? The PP? The PSOE? Was it you? Was it them? It was everyone. It was no one. No one ever shows up when it matters.

And worse yet—they’re expensive. And they rob us.

Enough is enough. If you’re tired of being robbed, lied to, and ignored, then you’re not alone.

This project isn’t signed by a politician. It’s signed by a common Spaniard.
